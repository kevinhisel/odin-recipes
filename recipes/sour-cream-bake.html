Sour Cream Bake

Description

This is a recipe for Sour Cream Bake. It is a easy to prepare casserole dish borrowed from The Pioneer Woman.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1 can 15-ounces tomato sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 8 ounces egg noodles
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1 1/4 cup small curd cottage cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced green onions
  • 1 cup grated sharp cheddar cheese

Steps

Prepare meat sauce

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Brown ground chuck in a large skillet.
  3. Drain fat, then add tomato sauce, 1/2 teaspoon salt and plenty of freshly ground black pepper.
  4. Stir, then simmer while you prepare the other ingredients.

Prepare other ingredients

  1. Cook egg noodles until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine sour cream and cottage cheese. Add plenty of freshly ground black pepper.
  3. Add to noodles and stir. Add green onions and stir.

Assemble the casserole

  1. To assemble, add half of the noodles to a baking dish.
  2. Top with half the meat mixture, then sprinkle on half the grated cheddar.
  3. Repeat with noodles, meat, then a final layer of cheese.
  4. Bake for 20 minutes, or until all cheese is melted.
